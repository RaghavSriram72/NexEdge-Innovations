    WE ARE NEXEDGE INNOVATIONS

    NexEdge Innovations is a team of high-schoolers based in Carmel, Indiana who aims to promote fundamental skills in education and views on global issues. We aspire to help other high-school students by creating a platform where they can share their opinions, ideas, and views on global issues, all while giving advice to students and inspiring them to learn and teach others.
